WHAT IS SAFEDIAL WIRELESS?

SafeDial Wireless is a unique Multi-Path 4G & IP wireless module that sits inside your existing alarm panel. The only full featured unit that can deliver alarm events to ANY Control Room on a secure and fully redundant platform.

EVENT DELIVERY VIA PSTN OR IP

Globe’s GL45-4G unit delivers alarm events to your Control Room via PSTN receivers via your 1345/1300 Security Number or via the Permaconn IP Multiline Receiver.

DIRECT EVENTS & SYSTEM ALERTS

All alarm events and system alerts are delivered in Contact ID directly to your Control Room, with fixed 4 hour integrity polling on 4G and IP.
